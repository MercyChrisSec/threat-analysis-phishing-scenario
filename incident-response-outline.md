# Incident Response Outline – Phishing Attack Scenario

## Project Context
This incident response outline corresponds to a simulated phishing attack targeting a small healthcare organization.  
It demonstrates structured steps to detect, contain, eradicate, recover, and learn from the incident.

---

## 1. Detection
- Staff reports suspicious emails to IT/security team.  
- Unusual login attempts or failed login alerts are monitored.  
- Email filtering or endpoint monitoring detects malicious attachments or links.

---

## 2. Containment
- Immediately disable any compromised accounts.  
- Isolate devices that opened malicious links or attachments.  
- Block sender addresses and URLs to prevent further spread.  

---

## 3. Eradication
- Remove phishing emails from all user inboxes.  
- Reset passwords for affected accounts and enforce MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication).  
- Scan devices for malware and remove any detected threats.  

---

## 4. Recovery
- Restore secure access to accounts and systems once confirmed clean.  
- Monitor systems and accounts for unusual activity.  
- Validate email filters and endpoint protection effectiveness.  

---

## 5. Lessons Learned
- Conduct targeted phishing awareness training for staff.  
- Review and update password policies to ensure strong credentials.  
- Improve email filtering rules and endpoint security measures.  
- Document the incident to refine future response plans.
